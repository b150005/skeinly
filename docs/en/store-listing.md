## Full Description

### English

Knit Note is the knitting companion app that helps you stay organized and connected.

**Track Your Projects**
Keep all your knitting projects in one place. Track your current row with a simple tap counter, add notes and photos to record your progress, and never lose your place again.

**Manage Your Patterns**
Build your personal pattern library with detailed metadata — gauge, yarn weight, needle size, and chart images. Search and filter to find exactly what you need.

**Discover & Fork**
Browse public patterns shared by the community. Found something inspiring? Fork it to your library and make it your own.

**Share Your Work**
Share patterns and projects with friends. Leave comments, follow activity feeds, and celebrate each other's progress.

**Works Offline**
Create projects and track progress even without an internet connection. Your data syncs automatically when you're back online.

**Features:**
- Row counter with increment/decrement
- Project management (create, edit, track status)
- Pattern library with search, filter, and sort
- Progress notes with photo attachments
- Public pattern discovery and forking
- Sharing with comments and activity feeds
- User profiles
- Offline-first with cloud sync
- Available on iPhone, iPad, and Android
