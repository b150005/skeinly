Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-05-12

You can delete your Skeinly account at any time. The fastest way is from within the app — the in-app flow runs the deletion as your authenticated session, so there is no waiting period and no manual identity verification. This page also describes a web-based fallback for cases where you cannot access the app.

Looking to clear your content but keep your account? Use Delete All My Data instead. That removes your patterns, projects, charts, and comments while preserving your sign-in and Pro membership.

Option 1 — Delete from the app (recommended)

  1. Open Skeinly on your phone.
  2. Tap Settings (gear icon in the navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to Account.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Read the confirmation dialog, then confirm.

Your account is deleted immediately. You will be signed out and the app returns to the sign-in screen.

Option 2 — Web-based deletion request

Use this option only if you cannot sign in to the app (lost device, forgotten password, etc.). The web flow requires manual identity verification by our operator and takes up to 7 calendar days.

  1. Send an email to skeinly.app@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request.
  2. Send the email from the email address associated with your Skeinly account. This is the identity verification step — we cannot process a deletion request from a different address (per GDPR Article 12(6)).
  3. We will reply within 7 calendar days confirming the deletion. If we cannot verify your identity (e.g., the From address does not match any Skeinly account), we will reply asking for a verifiable channel.

What gets deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is removed from Skeinly's systems:

Data typeRemoval
Account record (email, sign-in metadata)Immediate
Profile (display name, bio, avatar)Immediate (cascades)
Patterns, projects, progress, charts, variationsImmediate (cascades)
Suggestions, comments, activities you authoredImmediate (cascades)
Shares (from you and to you)Immediate (cascades)
Push notification tokensImmediate (cascades)
Subscription record (Skeinly side)Immediate
Avatar image file (Skeinly Storage)Immediate
Sentry / PostHog data (if you opted in)Anonymous — you may request removal directly from Sentry / PostHog using your anonymous install UUID
Apple App Store / Google Play purchase historyOut of Skeinly's control — managed by Apple / Google; you may request removal directly from them
RevenueCat subscription analyticsOut of Skeinly's direct control; contact RevenueCat support if you want their records purged
GitHub Issues filed via in-app bug reportVisible publicly at github.com/b150005/skeinly/issues; request deletion individually if needed
Supabase database backupsAuto-expire per 7-day PITR retention

Deletion is permanent. Once your account is deleted, your patterns, projects, and progress cannot be recovered. If you have a pending Pro subscription, cancel it from Apple Subscriptions or Google Play before deletion to stop future charges (deleting the Skeinly account does NOT cancel Apple/Google IAP subscriptions).

Cancel your Pro subscription separately (if applicable)

Deleting your Skeinly account does NOT automatically cancel your Apple App Store or Google Play subscription. Cancel directly with the store before (or after) deleting your account:

  • iOS: apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions or Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on the device.
  • Android: play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions or Play Store → Profile → Payments & subscriptions.

If you change your mind

Once an account is deleted, it cannot be restored. You are free to sign up again with the same email address — this will create a new account with no link to the deleted one.

Questions or issues with deletion? Email skeinly.app@gmail.com.
