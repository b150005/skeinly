Delete All My Data

Last updated: 2026-05-15

This page describes how to clear all your patterns, projects, charts, and comments while keeping your Skeinly account. Your sign-in, display name, avatar, and Pro membership stay intact — only the content you authored is removed.

Want to delete your account entirely instead? Use Account Deletion — that removes the account itself, not just the content.

What this deletes vs preserves

Deleted

  • Patterns you authored
  • Projects and progress
  • Charts, variations, version history
  • Comments, suggestions, suggestion comments
  • Shared links you created
  • Activity history
  • Downloaded symbol packs (re-downloadable)
  • Push notification tokens (re-registered on next sign-in)
  • Blocks you set (the block list, not the user)

Preserved

  • Sign-in (email and password or OAuth identity)
  • Display name and bio
  • Avatar image
  • Pro membership (if applicable)
  • Push notification preferences (re-applied automatically)
  • Account creation date

Option 1 — Delete from the app (recommended)

  1. Open Skeinly on your phone.
  2. Tap Settings (gear icon in the navigation bar).
  3. Scroll to Danger Zone.
  4. Tap Delete all my data.
  5. Read the preservation-matrix dialog, tap Continue.
  6. Type the confirmation phrase exactly as shown, then submit.

Your data is deleted immediately and a confirmation banner appears in Pattern Library. You stay signed in, and your Pro membership keeps working.

Option 2 — Web-based deletion request

Use this option only if you cannot sign in to the app (lost device, forgotten password, etc.). The web flow requires manual identity verification by our operator and takes up to 7 calendar days.

  1. Send an email to skeinly.app@gmail.com with the subject line Data Deletion Request (account-preserved).
  2. Send the email from the email address associated with your Skeinly account. This is the identity verification step — we cannot process a deletion request from a different address (per GDPR Article 12(6)).
  3. We will reply within 7 calendar days confirming the deletion. If we cannot verify your identity (e.g., the From address does not match any Skeinly account), we will reply asking for a verifiable channel.

Deletion is permanent. Once your patterns, projects, charts, and comments are deleted, they cannot be recovered. This action is intentionally distinct from account deletion — if you also want your account itself removed, use the account deletion flow.

How this relates to account deletion

Skeinly offers two related but distinct privacy controls:

  • Delete all my data (this page) keeps your account active and your Pro membership intact, but removes everything you authored. Useful when you want a "fresh start" without losing your sign-in.
  • Delete account removes your account record and your authored content together. Use this if you no longer want a Skeinly account at all.

You can run "Delete all my data" multiple times without consequence — it is idempotent. Each run deletes whatever content exists at the moment of the call.

If you change your mind

Once data is deleted, it cannot be restored. If you want to recover individual patterns or projects later, please cancel before submitting. After the deletion, you can sign back into Skeinly and start fresh.

Questions or issues with deletion? Email skeinly.app@gmail.com.
